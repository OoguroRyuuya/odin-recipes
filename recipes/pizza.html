Pizza

Description

This is a Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salami
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Add Yeast to Water
  2. Add sugar to Water
  3. Let yeast sit for 5 minutes
  4. Add Yeast water mixture to Flour
  5. Knead well
  6. Let the dough sit for 1-24h
  7. Roll out the dough in circle like shapes
  8. Put sauce,cheese and salami on the dough
  9. Bake in the oven for 5 minutes at 220 C
